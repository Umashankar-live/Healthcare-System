Our Story

As we have got this project , we have made use of most of the modern and efficient stack with a clean and aesthetically designed UI which will help manage assets of a company in an easy and efficient way. Usually there are many ambiguities in asset management systems like an asset is assigned to two different employees . We have designed a system where if any of the employee of the company needs an asset of the company , manager will raise request on his behalf. If manager tries to raise request for employee which does not exist, system will automatically reject the request. The administrator of the system has the authority to approve the request or reject it based on various parameters like checking if the requested asset is unallocated, remark is valid and if the employee exists .
